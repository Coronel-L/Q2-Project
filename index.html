The Philippines is a beautiful country that has many destinations that are worth your time and money. From beaches and its beautiful waters to mountains and refreshing greenery - all of these can be found in the Philippines.

Here are 3 of the best destinations in the Philippines!


Puerto Princesa

  • Puerto Princesa is the capital city of Palawan.
  • The most popular spot to visit in Puerto Princesa is its Underground River.
  • You even have the opportunity to explore the river and its wonders!

Boracay

  • Boracay is well known for its beaches, specifically, the White Beach.
  • It is perfect to go here during the summer to bathe in the sunlight while enjoying the fresh water.
  • Boracay also offers some of the best resorts for you to check-in during your stay!

Siargao

  • Cloud 9 has become one of the favorite spot of travelers for its beautiful features.
  • If you are into surfing, this is the best place in the Philippines to do so as it is said to be the surfing capital of the country!
